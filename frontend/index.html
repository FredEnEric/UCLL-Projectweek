About us

Everybody dreams of a car, but do you know what happened to the car that you want to buy?
We do!

We at AutoBlocks know with a 100% certainty the story of your car.
We provide software that keeps track of all the parts in your car using the blockchain technology. By using our solution the owner is ensured to get a high-quality part when the garage repairs your car. The system rejects counterfeit parts or parts that are not from a preferred vendor. Dealers can see what garages are using the right parts. Insurance companies can check which parts are in the car so only legitimate parts can be used when using AutoBlocks. When you're planning to buy a secondhand car you can see a full history of the parts in the car and the ones that were replaced. By using blockchain track of a full history of the parts in your car
